Customer Profiler

Life Stage Classification

Rule-based classification: new_graduate, young_family, mid_career, pre_retirement, retired

Risk Tolerance Scoring

Weighted multi-factor score (1-10) from age, income, investments, savings ratio, and leverage

Segment Derivation

Auto-segments: high_net_worth, strong_saver, leveraged based on feature thresholds

Offer Ranker

MiFID II Suitability Filter

Hard filter blocks mismatched risk levels before scoring. Credit products blocked for fragile customers.

Business Rules Engine

20 profiling rules from regulatory workbook. Context detection rules match products to customer signals.

Diversity Constraints

Product type diversity ensures balanced recommendations across savings, investments, insurance, and credit.

Customer Journey

Onboarding Wizard

Three steps from registration to personalized offers

1

Consent & Compliance

AI Act transparency notice, GDPR consent toggles (profiling, automated decisions, marketing channels, family data), and regulatory disclosures from the consent registry.

GDPR Art. 6 · GDPR Art. 22 · AI Act Art. 13

2

Profile Questionnaire

Age, annual income, dependents, employment status, risk tolerance, homeowner status, and existing banking products. Used by the profiler to classify life stage and risk score.

Life Stage · Risk Score · Segments

3

Review & Confirm

Full summary of consent selections and profile data. On confirmation, the system generates a computed profile and the customer immediately receives personalized offers.

Auto Profile · Instant Offers

Privacy by design: New customers receive auto-increment IDs (51, 52, ...). Email is never stored — only an irreversible SHA-256 hash. Internal IDs are replaced with pseudonymized UUIDs for external use. Data is automatically anonymized after 2 years.

Consent Registry

Excel Workbook Sync

Reads 5 sheets (Official Texts, Product Checkboxes, AI Rules, Implementation Map, Sources) with SHA-256 change detection.

Background Sync (6h)

Auto-increments version only on actual changes. Tracks sync history with timestamps and record counts.

9 Consent Types, 65 Product Mappings

Covers 12 products across GDPR, MiFID II, IDD, and AI Act requirements.

Regulatory Monitoring

EUR-Lex URL Monitoring

Fetches 6 EU regulation URLs every 24 hours and computes SHA-256 content hashes to detect legislative changes.

Admin Alert System

Red animated banner in admin portal when regulatory source changes detected. Dismissible "Mark as Reviewed" flow.

6 Tracked Regulations

GDPR, MiFID II, IDD, ePrivacy Directive, EU AI Act, EBA Guidelines — each with direct EUR-Lex links.
